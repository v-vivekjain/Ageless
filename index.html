Frequently Asked Questions

What if my parent isn't comfortable being interviewed?

The conversation is relaxed and guided, and we move at their pace. No preparation or writing is required.

What if memories are incomplete?

The process focuses on meaningful experiences and stories, not perfect timelines.

How long does it take?

The interview takes about an hour. The finished story is delivered within 10 days.

Who is this usually for?

Most often adult children arrange this as a gift for their parents or grandparents.

Can multiple family members contribute?

Yes. Additional input can be included during the process.
